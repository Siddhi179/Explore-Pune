About Pune

Pune is a vibrant city known for its rich history, educational institutions, and vibrant culture. It's a city that perfectly blends tradition and modernity.

Famous Tourist Spots

1.Shaniwar Wada

A historical fortification in the city of Pune, Shaniwar Wada was built in 1732.

It was served as the seat of the Peshwas of the Maratha Empire.

2.Sinhagad Fort

Sinhagad is a hill fortress located at around 35 km southwest of Pune.

It offers panoramic views of the surrounding region and has a rich history.

3.Dagdusheth Halwai Ganapati Temple

A revered temple in Pune, dedicated to the Hindu God Ganesh.

It is visited by thousands of devotees every year.

4.Aga Khan Palace

A majestic building in Pune which holds great historical significance.

Mahatma Gandhi and his wife were imprisoned here during the Quit India Movement.
